Mr. Robert Norman Ross

Here's a time line of Mr. Bob Ross life:

  • 1942 - Bob Ross was born in Daytona Beach, Florida. His birthday is October 29th.
  • 1961 - Bob enlisted for US Air Force at age of 18.
  • 1981 - Bob gets his hair permed to save monet on haircuts.
  • 1983 - The Joy of Painting's first season was produced by WNVC in Falls Church, Virginia, but the audience was small. And the quality of the audio and video was so poor, that the first season of The Joy of Painting was never aired again, and a book that goes along with it is a rare find. The partnership with WNVC dissolved.
  • 1990 - The Joy of Painting's 300th episode aired in the United States, and then in Canada. Soon, The Joy of Painting began broadcasting and dubbed in different languages around the world, such as Mexico, Costa Rica, Colombia, the United Kingdom, Europe, Latin America, the Netherlands, Greece, Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Turkey, Iran, South Korea, and Japan.
  • 1995 - Bob Ross died from lymphoma at age of 52.
